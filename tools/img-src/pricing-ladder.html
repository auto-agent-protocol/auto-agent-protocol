Four pricing fields, one honest number
msrp
manufacturer’s suggested retail (the sticker)
↘
list_price
dealer’s advertised price
↘
offered_price
list_price + taxes for the buyer’s zip (when provided)
↘
FTC-FINAL
price
out-the-door amount the buyer can actually pay — fees included
price is the only field a buyer agent may quote as final  ·  all prices are plain integers (USD)
